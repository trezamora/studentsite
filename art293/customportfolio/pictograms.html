Olympic Pictograms

Pictogram/Ideogram Design

For Olympic Pictograms, I was tasked with creating a set of at least 4 pictograms that represent various events in the Olympic Games. Having the choice to choose four Olympic Games, the pictograms needed to communicate what they represented in a clear and unified way. Being a huge fan of the Olympics and sports, I was excited to take on the project. For my four Olympic Games I chose to represent Archery, Basketball, Baseball, and Golf.

Filtering through various examples from past Olympic years, I wanted to put my own twist on how the games could be represented. Given a short turnaround time, the outcome for this project was four unique typographic inspired pictograms that incorporated the sport into the final design of my pictograms.
