Testimonials

Ryan

I was diagnosed with “Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia” 2 days before my 5th birthday back on November 2nd, 1995. I am currently in pursuit of a growing passion of mine, RAP! I'm so in love with all the components and the entire process of creating a song. I love to write, I love to help, and I love to create… I started getting into rap back in 2008 when a friend of mine, “Corissa Lynn Namahana Bueno-Kanei” passed on from an infection… after defeating cancer multiple times, but an infection took my friend away from me… So then… A mutual friend and I wanted to pay homage with a song dedicated to beautiful Corissa that I still have yet to complete…

“Everyone has their own cancer to battle, never let that cancer define you.”
